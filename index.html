About Us

We Are A Trusted Company With One Year Of Experience

We provide loans to individual as well as group members in the under developed regions of India for a range of income-generating activities. Lokkalyan Microcare Foundation serves thousands of poor people across villages and urban slums in India. By providing the poors with wide range of economic tools, we are looking forward to the eradication of poverty.

  • Income Generating Loan

    The Company offers loans for income-generating activities such as livestock, agriculture, trading and production businesses

  • Individual Lending

    Under individual lending, loan officers bear principle responsibility for loan decisions; they screen, and monitor their clients as well as come up with mechanisms of enforcing repayment.

  • Utility Loans

    From Open Energy Information. Loan programs provide financing for the purchase of renewable energy or energy efficiency systems or equipment.

  • Group Lending

    A lending mechanism which allows a group of individuals - often called a solidarity group to provide collateral or loan guarantee through a group repayment pledge
