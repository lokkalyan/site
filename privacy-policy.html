Lokkalyan Microcare Foundation offers you multiple payment methods. Whatever your online mode of payment, you can trust assured that Lokkalyan Microcare Foundation trusted payment gateway partners use secure encryption technology to keep your transaction details confidential at all times.

You may use Internet Banking, Debit Card, Credit Card and Cash on Delivery to make your purchase. We also accept payments made using Visa, MasterCard, American Express and Any Club credit/debit cards.

You can make credit card payments through the Lokkalyan Microcare Foundation mobile site. Lokkalyan Microcare Foundation uses 256-bit encryption technology to protect your card information while securely transmitting it to the secure and trusted payment gateways managed by leading banks.
